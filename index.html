Our Services

Web Design

Web sites must be highly detailed oriented and creative. The most important component of a website is the initial impression a user gets.

UI/UX Design

User experience is important to us. From user experience research to prototyping, we love designing processes to better understand how your users really experience your business.

Social Media

Everyone knows social media is important, but figuring out how to use it is a different story. We create content targeted to the audiences that are most relevant to your business.

Logo Design

We love branding, and the heart of a brand is the logo. We use all the tools of design to make sure your logo truly represents you.

Projects

  • Fernando created branding and a one page website to highlight this fictional construction company. He used clean lines and strong shapes in the logo to highlight the business without using conventional imagery associated with construction.

  • Emma designed a website and logo for the London Youth Advisory Council's election season. She built off pre-existing branding to design a tertiary colour for the organization and integrate the leaf imagery into the ballot box.

  • Fernando crafted a detailed graffiti illustration with Kanye West at its centre. Its bold and colourful design aligns with the spirit of graffiti.

  • Emma designed and coded a website for a fictional home decor store built around her own photography. This branding highlights the white colour scheme and the feminine quirkiness of the store.

  • Fernando designed and coded a website to commemorate the 15th anniversary of Lord of the Rings. This updated branding incorporates a flat logo design and places emphasis on the ring.

  • Emma collaborated with a team to create a design and branding for a London area apple orchard. This logo focuses on the colourful family friendly feel of the business.
